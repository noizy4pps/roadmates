Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026

1. Who we are
RoadMates is operated by Noizyapps. Contact: support@noizyapps.com

2. What data we collect
Data	Purpose
Email address	Account creation and login
Name	Display to other users (optional)
GPS location	Showing you on the live map while visibility is enabled
Vehicle make, model, type	Map marker display
License plate number	Identity on the map; stored hashed for search
Device push token	Wave and invite notifications
Phone number	Optional contact sharing with other users

3. How we use your data

    To operate the live map and connect you with nearby travelers
    To send notifications (waves, invites) you have opted into
    To verify your vehicle via the KYC selfie process (image processed by OpenAI Vision, not stored after verification)
    To enforce our Terms of Service and prevent abuse

4. Data sharing
We share your data only as follows:

    Other RoadMates users — your vehicle type, country, partial plate, and location (when visible) are visible to other users on the map
    OpenAI — selfie images submitted for KYC verification are sent to OpenAI's API for plate recognition and immediately discarded
    Push notification providers — your device token is used solely to deliver in-app notifications
    We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers.

5. Location data
Your GPS coordinates are transmitted to our servers only while you are visible on the map. When you toggle visibility off, location sharing stops. We do not store a history of your location movements.

6. Data retention
Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time via Settings → Account → Delete account. Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed within 30 days.

7. Your rights
Depending on your jurisdiction you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@noizyapps.com.

8. Children
RoadMates is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Security
We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. No system is completely secure; use the App at your own risk.

10. Changes
We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of material changes via the App.
