# roadmates
Find fellow travelers on a live map. Share your plate. Connect on the road.


Ever passed another overlander, campervan, or road tripper and thought — I wish I could catch up with them?

RoadMates is a live map for travelers. Share your GPS location alongside your vehicle and license plate, discover who else is moving near you, and connect in real time — no social media, no followers, no algorithm. Just a map full of people who are actually out there.

HOW IT WORKS

When you open RoadMates, your vehicle appears on a shared live map. Other travelers nearby can see you, and you can see them — identified by vehicle type, license plate, and country flag. Tap any marker to learn more, send a wave, or propose a meetup point.

It's built for the moment you're moving: simple, fast, and focused on real-world connection.

FEATURES

📍 Live map — See nearby travelers in real time. Markers update automatically as people move.

🚗 Vehicle filter — Filter the map by vehicle type. See only RVs, only motorcycles, only caravans — whatever matches your tribe.

👋 Send a wave — A quick, ephemeral ping to let someone know you see them. Nothing stored, nothing logged.

📌 Invite to your location — Share your exact GPS coordinates as a meetup point.

🗺️ Invite to a POI — Pick a nearby point of interest — gas station, restaurant, trailhead, campsite — and send it as a meetup invitation.

🔍 Plate search — Look up any license plate to see if that vehicle is a RoadMate.

👁️ Visibility toggle — Go invisible whenever you want. Your marker disappears from the map instantly.

🔒 Plate privacy — Your full plate is masked by default. You control who sees it.

🌍 8 languages — English, Italian, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Russian, Chinese.

WHO IS IT FOR?

RoadMates is for anyone who travels by vehicle and believes the best part of the road is the people you meet on it.

    Overlanders and off-road travelers
    Vanlifers and full-time nomads
    RV travelers and caravanners
    Motorcycle tourers
    Road trippers of all kinds

Whether you're crossing a continent or spending a weekend on a trail, RoadMates makes it possible to find your people — in the moment, exactly where you are.

BUILT FOR THE ROAD, NOT THE FEED

No timeline. No likes. No followers. No content to scroll through.

RoadMates does one thing: it shows you who's nearby, right now, and gives you a simple way to connect. When you close the app, you're invisible again. It's designed to enhance real-world encounters, not replace them.

PRIVACY

Your location is only shared while you choose to be visible. You can toggle off at any time. Your full license plate is masked by default — other users see only a partial plate unless you choose to reveal it. We do not sell your data.

CURRENTLY IN OPEN BETA

RoadMates is actively developed. We're a small team building this because we believe accidental encounters on the road are worth keeping. Your feedback shapes what comes next — leave a review or reach out directly.
